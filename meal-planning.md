## Weekly meal plan prompt

> "Plan my meals for the week. Family of four. Preferences and constraints are in `workflows/meal-planning.md`. Give me:
> - Five weeknight dinners (quick to moderate — under 45 minutes)
> - One weekend dinner (can be more involved)
> - One batch/prep suggestion for Sunday
> - A consolidated shopping list grouped by category
>
> Vary the cuisines. Don't repeat proteins across consecutive days."
