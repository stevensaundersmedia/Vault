# Workflow: Weekly Schedule

## Purpose
I use AI to help plan my week, balancing professional output with family commitments, gym sessions, and golf. This workflow ensures nothing gets dropped and the week has the right shape.

## Weekly planning prompt

Use this at the start of each week (Sunday evening or Monday morning):

> "Help me plan this week. My standing commitments are in `workflows/weekly-schedule.md`. Here is what I need to get done this week: [list]. Here are any specific constraints or events: [note]. Build me a day-by-day structure that gives me focused work blocks, protects family time, and doesn't try to cram everything into Monday."

---

## Principles for a well-shaped week

- Deep work blocks in the morning where possible
- Family commitments treated as fixed, not flexible
- One admin block per day maximum — batch emails, scheduling, admin together
- At least one proper off-switch each day — not just stopping, but a clear end point
- Golf and gym are not optional extras — they are scheduled like meetings

---

## Weekly review prompt

Use this on Friday afternoon:

> "Here's what I planned to do this week [paste plan]. Here's what actually happened [brief notes]. What carried over? What does next week need to absorb? What pattern am I noticing?"
