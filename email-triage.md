## Triage prompt

When I paste or forward emails for triage, use this logic:

> "Read these emails from my personal inboxes. For each one:
> 1. Extract any action required from me
> 2. Note any deadline, explicit or implied
> 3. Flag anything time-sensitive (within 48 hours)
> 4. Flag anything that relates to an open project or opportunity in the vault
> 5. Suggest a reply if one is needed — draft in my voice per `me/voice.md`
>
> Output a clean action list, then list anything that needs no action but is worth noting."

---

## Reply principles

- Replies should be in my voice — read `me/voice.md` before drafting
- Do not over-explain or over-apologise
- Match the register of the incoming email — if it's casual, don't be formal
- Personal emails to close contacts should feel like they came from me, not a tool

---

## What does NOT come through this workflow

- Work email — handled entirely separately, not in the vault
- Financial correspondence — extract action only, no detail stored
- Anything touching family members beyond "my wife / my eldest / my youngest"
