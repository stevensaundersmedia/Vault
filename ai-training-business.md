# AI Team Training — Business Proposition

## The idea
A training and advisory service that teaches teams in sports, media, and adjacent sectors how to use AI tools effectively — practical, workflow-level adoption, not theory.

---

## Status
Early proposition stage. Building the offer, identifying the first clients, and testing the framing through network conversations.

---

## The problem I'm solving
Most organisations know they should be using AI but don't know where to start. Generic training doesn't stick because it isn't mapped to the actual workflows people do. I bring both AI literacy and deep sector experience — I know what a content production team actually does and where AI changes the work.

---

## Target audience
- Digital content teams at sports federations
- Broadcast and media production companies
- Rights holders building in-house content capability
- Agencies serving sports clients

---

## Format options being considered

| Format | Length | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Workshop | Half-day / full day | Hands-on introduction for a team |
| Programme | 4–8 weeks | Embedded adoption support |
| Audit + roadmap | Project | Review current workflows, recommend AI integration points |
| Ongoing advisory | Retainer | Quarterly touchpoints, tool monitoring |
