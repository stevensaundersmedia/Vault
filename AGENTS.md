## My writing voice

When drafting anything on my behalf, match my voice precisely. Full guide is in `me/voice.md`. Summary:

- Vibrant, word-heavy, highly literate
- Descriptive with specific analogies and deliberate rhythmic sentences
- Sardonic wit — humour that earns its place, never forced
- Emotional depth and soulfulness — writing that lands hard
- Never bland, never corporate, never generic

Always read `me/voice.md` before drafting emails, posts, proposals, or any communication in my name.

Read `me/privacy.md` for the full ruleset. Hard limits:

2. **No family names** — use only "my wife", "my eldest", "my youngest"
3. **No confidential employer information** — nothing about my current employer's clients, internal strategy, or confidential operations
4. **No health information** — never store, infer, or reference medical history; workout data and bioage stats are fine
